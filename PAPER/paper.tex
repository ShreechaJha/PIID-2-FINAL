\begin{abstract}
This paper presents a multi-agent framework designed to handle unstructured text and image data, perform optical character recognition (OCR), detect Personally Identifiable Information (PII), assess cumulative privacy risk, and apply organizational governance policies.
\end{abstract}

\section{Introduction}
Personally Identifiable Information (PII) leakage represents a significant risk for modern digital platforms. Handing this risk requires active cleaning, visual validation, and compliance alignment.
